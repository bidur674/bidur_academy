Our Location

Bidur Academy is conveniently located in the heart of the Nawalpur district, easily accessible by public transportation and with parking facilities. Our modern campus features state-of-the-art computer labs, collaborative learning spaces, and a comprehensive library. We welcome prospective students and their families to visit our campus and experience our learning environment firsthand. Tours are available by appointment, and our staff will be happy to show you around and answer any questions you may have about our facilities, programs, and student life.
